Problem We Solve

Too many animals are living on the streets with no food, no shelter, no medical care, and zero cuddles. Many are hungry, sick and alone. Our mission is to change that.

Who Is Harmed?

  • Animals: Stray dogs and cats suffer from hunger, illness, injuries and lack of love or protection.
  • The Planet: Animal waste increases nitrogen and phosphorus in soil and water, harming ecosystems.

Why This Matters

Addressing the stray animal crisis gives animals safe homes and medical care, reduces safety risks to people, and protects our environment. Stray animals can scratch or bite and their waste can pollute soil and water, harming plants and wildlife. By solving this problem, we improve the lives of animals, people and the planet.
